1. Program Name

FTW Research SMS Survey Program

2. Program Description

FTW Research conducts SMS-based community surveys and public opinion polls. Messages are sent to registered voters and community members to gather opinions on policy issues, ballot measures, and other topics of public interest. Each survey consists of an SMS invitation containing a link to a web-based questionnaire.

3. Message Frequency

You will receive a maximum of 2 messages per survey project: one survey invitation and one optional follow-up reminder. This is not a recurring or subscription messaging program.

4. Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Please contact your wireless carrier for details about your text messaging plan and any associated costs.

5. How to Get Help

For help or questions about this messaging program, reply HELP to any message from FTW Research, or email wally@ftwresearch.com.

6. How to Opt Out

To stop receiving messages from FTW Research, reply STOP to any message at any time. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent. You may also email wally@ftwresearch.com to request removal.

7. Consent

By participating in an FTW Research survey, you acknowledge that you have read and agree to these terms and conditions and our Privacy Policy.
